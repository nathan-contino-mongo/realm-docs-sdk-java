io.realm.internal.objectstore
=============================

`java.lang.Object <https://docs.oracle.com/javase/7/docs/api/java/lang/Object.html>`__ 

Callback used when sending back results from network requests performed by ObjectStores ``realm::app::NetworkTransport`` . The callback will happen on the thread running the network request, not the intended receiver thread.
