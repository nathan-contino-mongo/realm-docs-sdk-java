io.realm.internal.jni
=====================

`java.lang.Object <https://docs.oracle.com/javase/7/docs/api/java/lang/Object.html>`__ 

Protocol for passing **BsonValue (?)**s to JNI.

For now this just encapsulated the BSON value in a document with key VALUE. This overcomes the shortcoming of ``org.bson.JsonWrite``  not being able to serialize single values.
