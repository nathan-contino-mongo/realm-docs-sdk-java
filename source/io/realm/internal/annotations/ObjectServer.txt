io.realm.internal.annotations
=============================

**Implemented interfaces:**



- `java.lang.annotation.Annotation <https://docs.oracle.com/javase/7/docs/api/java/lang/annotation/Annotation.html>`__ 


This annotation is used to mark the classes as being specific to the Realm Object Server. They will be stripped in the Base variant.
