io.realm.internal.log.obfuscator
================================

`java.lang.Object <https://docs.oracle.com/javase/7/docs/api/java/lang/Object.html>`__ 
 | 		:ref:`io.realm.internal.log.obfuscator.RegexPatternObfuscator <io_realm_internal_log_obfuscator_RegexPatternObfuscator>` 

Obfuscator for email- and password-related login requests.It will replace the



- ``"email":"<EMAIL>"`` ,
   
   
   
   
- ``"username":"<USERNAME>"``  and
   
   
   
   
- ``"password":"<PASSWORD>"`` 
   
   
   
   


patterns with

- ``"email":"***"`` ,
   
   
   
   
- ``"username":"***"``  and
   
   
   
   
- ``"password":"***"`` 
   
   
   
   


respectively.
