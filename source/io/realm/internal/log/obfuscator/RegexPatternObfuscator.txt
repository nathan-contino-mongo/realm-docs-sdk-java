io.realm.internal.log.obfuscator
================================

`java.lang.Object <https://docs.oracle.com/javase/7/docs/api/java/lang/Object.html>`__ 

The obfuscator removes sensitive information from logcat entries.Children classes have to provide a map of regex `Pattern <https://docs.oracle.com/javase/7/docs/api/java/util/regex/Pattern.html>`__ s and replacement strings to correctly hide the information.



For example, the following pattern finds instances of ``"token":"<TOKEN_GOES_HERE>"``  in the logcat: ``Pattern.compile("((\"token\"):(\".+?\"))")`` . And the replacement string ``"\"token\":\"***\""``  replaces those instances with ``"token":"***"`` .
