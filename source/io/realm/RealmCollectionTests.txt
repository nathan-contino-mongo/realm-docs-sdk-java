:ref:`io.realm.CollectionTests <io_realm_CollectionTests>` 
 | 		`java.lang.Object <https://docs.oracle.com/javase/7/docs/api/java/lang/Object.html>`__ 

Test class for all methods part of the the :ref:`RealmCollection <io_realm_RealmCollection>`  interface.
