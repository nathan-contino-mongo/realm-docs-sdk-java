       **Parameters**
       
       - ``token`` - the reset password token.
          
          
          
       - ``tokenId`` - the id of the reset password token.
          
          
          
       - ``newPassword`` - the new password for the user identified by the ``token`` . The password must be between 6 and 128 characters long.
